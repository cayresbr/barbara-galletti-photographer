The Photographer

Capturing the essence of life's most precious moments

Hello! I'm Barbara Galletti, a Brazilian photographer based in Utah. I specialize in family, maternity, wedding, and personal portrait photography. My approach is centered on capturing authenticity and emotional connection in every moment, creating timeless images that tell your unique story.

I believe photography goes beyond simply pressing a button – it's about creating a comfortable and memorable experience where you can truly be yourself.
